About:

What: Visualization of income inequality rate and economic freedom indicators.
Why: Is important to understand how different indicators of the economic freedom correlates with the income inequality rate.
How: You can see the income inequality column and indicators columns for each country by year.
You can select up to 15 countries and up to 5 different indicators to compare from the panels at the right.
You can select the year in which you are interested with the first slider at the bottom.
You can select a range of years for which the table will show the correlation between the income inequality rate and each selected economic freedom indicator.
